🧩Match and Mint

📝How to Play

  1. Choose a wallet: Injected (MetaMask/Backpack/etc.) or WalletConnect.
  2. Click “Injected Wallet” or “WalletConnect.”
  3. Then click “Start Game.”
  4. Rearrange pieces before the timer hits 0.
  5. Hit “Mint NFT” anytime until time’s up.
  6. If you don’t finish, your arrangement is your masterpiece—feel free to mint or Restart.
