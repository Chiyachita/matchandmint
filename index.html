🧩Match and Mint

📝How to Play

  1. Connect your wallet (MetaMask/Backpack/etc.).
  2. Click “Start Game” to load a 4×4 puzzle.
  3. Drag pieces to rearrange before the timer hits 0.
  4. Click “Mint NFT” to capture and mint your arrangement.
  5. If time runs out, mint your “masterpiece” or Restart.
