🧩 Match and Mint

📝 How to Play

  1. Connect your wallet using the "Connect Wallet" button.
  2. Ensure you’re on Monad Testnet (Chain ID 10143).
  3. Click "Start Game" to scramble a random puzzle.
  4. Rearrange the pieces before the timer (45s) runs out.
  5. If you finish early you can hit "Mint NFT" to lock in your design;
  6. When time’s up you’ll be prompted to mint your snapshot as an NFT.
